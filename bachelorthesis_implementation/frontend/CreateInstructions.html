Overview & Steps

In this job, you will be presented with four abstracts in total that are structured as
 a collection (three documents that are thematically similar) and 
 a single document (that thematically could be similar, different or intermediate to the collection). 

Your task is to read all documents, come up with keywords or key phrases and rate given keywords that describe the thematically commonalities or differences between the single document and the collection. 

Please follow all of these steps in order to submit a valid task (you can not go backwards):

  1. Answer all test questions regarding your English language niveau.
  2. Read the collection and the single document carefully (they will be displayed all along).
  3. Compare the read collection and the single document.
    3.1 What topics/themes do they have in common? Find appropriate keywords or key phrases for these common topics.
    3.2 What topics/themes are new in the single document and are not covered by the collection? Find appropriate keywords or key phrases for these new topics.
  4. Rate all given keywords or key phrases regarding their utility of describing the commonalities or differences between the read single document and the collection.
  5. Give us feedback to improve our task structure and submit your work.

Attention for non-native English speaker:  Please accept this task only if your English skills are at a specific niveau (at least CEFR Level: B2, IELTS Score: 5, TOEFL Score: 72) because the task requires certain language skills! Please have in mind that if the English language test answers are incorrect, your work will be rejected.
